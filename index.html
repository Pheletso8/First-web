About Me

I was born and raised in Soweto, where I completed my high school education at Meadowlands Secondary School. I then pursued a general BSc degree at the University of the Witwatersrand, majoring in Chemistry and Applied Chemistry, which I completed in 2022. After my undergraduate studies, I enrolled in the BSc Honours Chemistry program, completing the four traditional branches of Chemistry: Organic, Inorganic, Physical, and Analytical. I also undertook two research projects and studied contemporary topics, one of which— “Applications of AI in Chemistry”—sparked my passion for Artificial Intelligence and led me to transition into Data Science. The following year, I pursued a Postgraduate Diploma in Data Science at the same university. I studied the mathematical and statistical foundations of Data Science, principles of machine learning, and its practical applications. I applied these skills to small research projects in domains like finance and healthcare. Outside academics, I am passionate about sports. I enjoy swimming and playing futsal—a form of indoor football. I hope this gives you a better sense of who I am. Thank you for visiting!
